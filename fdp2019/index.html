Records of transactions, based on certain agreed upon conditions form the basis for exchanging assets between two parties (the frictions in the processes, such as ascertaining the real owner of the asset, capital/asset is locked for days until the transaction is cleared, still exists as they could not be solved by technology until now).

By maintaining an immutable, distributed ledger in a distributed network, Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) systems has the potential to reduce the friction in business networks and thus the operating cost for business transactions…

Blockchain being a disruptive technology provides immense opportunities to the government, industry, and academia to design and implement transparent solutions for many real-life problems. The technology is available beyond crypto-currencies for a broader variety of applications for a variety of sectors.

This FDP/Training program is designed for technology understanding – both at foundational and advanced level for the capacity building in Blockchain. Five days deliberations encompass technical talks, case studies, hands on, technology showcase demos and interactive discussions with eminent speakers and brilliant Blockchain programmers - presenting technology advancements, readiness and promising use cases.  

This program is supported by VJTI Mumbai, MeitY, TEQIP III – and open for the country wide capacity building in Blockchain and DLT.
